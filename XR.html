Range Extender Port - No

Keeping it simple! If you take away the shrink wrap on the cable coming out of the cable connector box, there are bullet connectors that will allow you to keep the connector port intact in case you change your mind later. Use some hot glue or another method to isolate the bullet connectors to keep them clean and dry. You can probably also fit any extra wiring into the plug protector box.
