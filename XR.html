Configuration Question 1

Keep the black appearance, or return to the classic XR look?

Strip off the old grip-tape and apply the brand new grip tape to give the board a real lift.

Strip off the camo rail guards to reveal the pristine naked rails. Replace the back and front footpads with the factory-new footpads included.

Configuration Question 2

Do you want to keep the range extender port?

Awesome. Then you probably also want to offer to buy the cord and battery connector.

Keeping it simple! If you take away the shrink wrap on the cable coming out of the cable connector box, there are bullet connectors that will allow you to keep the connector port intact in case you change your mind later. Use some hot glue or another method to isolate the bullet connectors to keep them clean and dry. You can probably also fit any extra wiring into the plug protector box.
